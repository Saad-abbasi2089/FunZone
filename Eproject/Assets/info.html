Fun Zone Park

Your place for joy, laughter, and adventure!

Park Story

Fun Zone Park started with a simple idea — to create a place where happiness never ends. What was once an empty land slowly turned into a colorful world filled with laughter, excitement, and unforgettable moments.

Every ride, light, and sound in Fun Zone Park was designed to bring people together. From thrilling adventures for the brave to joyful rides for families and kids, the park welcomes everyone with open arms.

Fun Zone Park is not just about entertainment — it’s about creating memories, celebrating life, and escaping daily stress. Each visit feels like a festival, and every smile tells a story.

Fun Zone Park is where fun comes alive and memories are made forever.

Park Rules

  • Entry ticket is required for all visitors
  • Children must be accompanied by adults
  • Follow ride height and age restrictions
  • Outside food and drinks are not allowed near rides
  • Keep the park clean — use trash bins
  • Pets are not allowed inside the park
  • Smoking and alcohol are strictly prohibited
  • Follow all safety instructions given by staff
  • Secure loose items before rides
  • Respect park staff, visitors, and property

Failure to follow rules may result in removal from the park.

The Family Behind Fun Zone Park

Fun Zone Park was proudly built by a family who believed that joy should be shared by everyone. What began as a family dream soon became a place loved by thousands.

The family wanted to create a safe, joyful, and welcoming environment where families could bond, children could laugh freely, and friends could make lifelong memories.

Every detail of Fun Zone Park reflects the family’s values — care, safety, happiness, and community. Their goal has always been simple: to give people a place where happiness feels endless.

Today, Fun Zone Park stands as a symbol of love, unity, and fun — built by a family, enjoyed by all

Visitor Information

Food Stalls

Enjoy delicious snacks, meals, and drinks at our dedicated food zones. Stalls are located near the main entrance, playground area, and central plaza. Vegetarian, kids-friendly, and healthy options are available.

Children’s Rights

  • Every child must be safe and supervised by an adult.
  • All rides follow age and height restrictions for safety.
  • Play areas are designed for children’s comfort and enjoyment.
  • First-aid assistance is available at all times for children.

Family Rights

  • Families can enjoy picnic areas, shaded seating, and rest zones.
  • Privacy and comfort are respected in family zones.
  • All families have equal access to attractions and activities.
  • Customer service is available to assist with family needs.
